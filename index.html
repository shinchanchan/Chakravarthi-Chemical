About Us

Leading Chemical
Manufacturer in Tamil Nadu

Chakravarthi Chemical is a premier manufacturer of industrial water treatment chemicals, dyeing auxiliaries, and specialty chemicals. Based in Perundurai, Tamil Nadu, we serve textile, pharmaceutical, food processing, and engineering industries across South India.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility produces high-performance chemicals for RO plants, boilers, cooling towers, ETP plants, and effluent treatment systems. All products are formulated to meet international quality standards.
